Constituency Development Fund - Zambia

Providing financial assistance to vulnerable groups including persons with disabilities, orphans, the elderly, students, and other vulnerable members of our society through constituency-based development initiatives.

Apply Now

Persons with Disabilities

Financial support for individuals with disabilities to improve their quality of life and promote inclusion.

Orphans

Assistance for orphans to ensure they have access to basic needs, education, and healthcare services.

Vulnerable Groups

Support for vulnerable individuals and families in our communities facing economic hardships.

Elderly

Financial aid for elderly citizens to help with medical expenses, food, and daily living needs.

Schools

Funding for schools in underserved communities to improve educational facilities and resources.

Students

Scholarships and bursaries for students from disadvantaged backgrounds to pursue education.
